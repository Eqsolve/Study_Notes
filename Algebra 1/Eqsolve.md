<h2>
  Eqation with the variable in the denominator
</h2>


$$
7 - \frac {10}{x} = 2 + \frac {15}{x}
$$

<p align="center">
  Solution
</p>

$$
x(7 - \frac {10}{x}) = x(2 + \frac {15}{x})
$$ 


$$
7x -10 = 2x + 15  
$$

$$
\frac {7x - 2x}{7 - 2} = \frac{15+10}{7-2}= 5
$$

$$
x = 5  
$$
